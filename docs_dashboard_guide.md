## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Executive Summary

**Purpose:** High-level business overview

**Key Metrics:**
- Total Revenue
- Total Transactions
- Average Order Value
- Profit Margin

**Visualizations:**
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top Categories by Revenue
- Payment Method Distribution
- Geographic Sales Map

**Usage:**
- Use date slicer to filter time range
- Click categories to drill down

---

### Page 2: Product Analysis

**Purpose:** Product performance insights

**Key Insights:**
- Top 10 products generate majority of revenue
- Electronics category shows highest profitability
- Discount impact analysis

**Visualizations:**
- Product revenue ranking
- Category-wise profit margins
- Units sold by product

---

### Page 3: Customer Insights

**Purpose:** Customer behavior analysis

**Key Insights:**
- Small percentage of customers generate large revenue share
- High-value customers show repeat purchase behavior
- Customer lifetime value distribution

**Visualizations:**
- Customer segmentation
- CLV distribution
- Repeat vs new customers

---

### Page 4: Geographic & Trends

**Purpose:** Location and temporal patterns

**Key Insights:**
- Top 5 states contribute majority of revenue
- Weekend sales outperform weekdays
- Seasonal purchase trends observed

**Visualizations:**
- State-wise revenue map
- Sales by weekday
- Monthly growth trends

---

## Filters Available

- Date Range
- Product Category
- Customer State
- Payment Method

These filters update all visualizations dynamically.

---

## Refreshing Data

1. Run the pipeline to generate fresh analytics CSVs
2. Open Power BI / Tableau
3. Refresh data source connections
4. Publish updated dashboard if required

---

## Business Insights & Recommendations

- Focus marketing on high-value customer segments
- Expand product lines in top-performing categories
- Optimize promotions during high-conversion periods
- Improve logistics in underperforming regions
